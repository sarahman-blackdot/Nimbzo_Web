Delete Account

At Nimbzo, we value your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account, we are here to assist you.

How to Request Account Deletion

Please follow these steps to permanently delete your Nimbzo account:

  1. Open your email client.
  2. Compose a new email to support@nimbzo.com.
  3. Use the subject line: Delete Account.
  4. Send the email from the address associated with your Nimbzo account so we can verify your identity.

Data Deletion & Retention Policy

We believe in transparency regarding your data. Here is what happens when you request deletion:

  • Deleted Data: Your account profile, personal information, travel plans, social posts, comments, and connection history will be permanently removed from our active databases.
  • Retained Data: We may retain correspondence relating to your deletion request for our administrative records. Some anonymized, non-personal usage data may be retained for analytical purposes to improve our service.
  • Retention Period: We process all valid deletion requests within 7 days. Once processed, your data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

If you have any questions before proceeding, please contact our support team.
