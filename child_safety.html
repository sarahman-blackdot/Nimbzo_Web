Nimbzo Child Safety Standards

Nimbzo has a zero-tolerance policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). We are committed to protecting minors and strictly prohibit the upload, sharing, or distribution of any content that exploits or endangers children.

Our Measures

  • Reporting: Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the app via the "Report" feature.
  • Moderation: We employ a combination of automated detection and human moderation to review reported content.
  • Action: Any account found sharing CSAM or violating our child safety policies will be immediately and permanently banned, and relevant data will be reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and law enforcement as required by law.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact our support team.
